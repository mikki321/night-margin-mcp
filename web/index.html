night-margin

What your calendar hides: the profit left after every turnover cost.




Property heatmap

Color = net € per available night — red is a loss, gray near zero, deeper blue is better. Thin bar = occupancy. Tap or hover a tile for details.
Table view

Triage — net-negative bookings

Bookings whose gross does not cover their own turnover cost. Each one is money lost while the calendar looks “full”. Tap a row to locate the property on the map.

Pricing decisions

Gap-night floor decisions from the local decision log. “Propose now” scans the next 30 days for gap nights priced below your cost floor (turnover + travel + minimum margin). Preview always shows the exact payload first — nothing is written without an explicit confirm.

Alerts

Checks gap nights priced below your cost floor in the next 30 days, plus new bookings since the last check. Running it here never sends a notification — but it does record the bookings it sees as seen, so a scheduled check_alerts will not re-alert about them.
Run it on your data: claude mcp add margin -- npx -y night-margin-mcp
